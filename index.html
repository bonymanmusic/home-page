About Bony Man

Icelandic singer/songwriter Bony Man (Guðlaugur Jón Árnason) creates music that is finely tuned, honest, and completely timeless. His latest work, Cinnamon Fields, captures the essence of his soulful storytelling and exceptional vocal range.
